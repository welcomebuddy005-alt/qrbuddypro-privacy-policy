Privacy Commitment

At QRBuddy Pro, your privacy is important to us. We designed our app to keep your data on your device whenever possible.

1. Data Storage

Your scan history, generated QR codes, and favorites are stored locally on your device. We do not maintain servers that store your personal scan data.

2. Camera Permission

Camera access is used only for QR and barcode scanning. Camera frames are processed in-memory and are never uploaded or recorded.

3. Files & Media Access

Files and media permissions are used only when importing or saving QR images and documents.

4. No Tracking

QRBuddy Pro does not sell user data or use invasive tracking technologies.

5. Security

We use modern Android technologies including CameraX and Google ML Kit for secure and efficient processing.
